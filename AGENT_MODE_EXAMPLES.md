**Current Situation:**

**How Local Dental SEO Works:**

Three factors determine local rankings:
1. **Relevance** - Does Google know you're a dentist in Austin?
2. **Distance** - How close are you to the searcher?
3. **Prominence** - Are you well-reviewed and trusted?

### Agent Mode Example:
**User:** "Research keywords for content marketing"

**Agent Mode Response:**
```

**Context I need:**
- Are you a content marketing agency (need to rank for services)?
- Are you teaching content marketing (informational content)?
```
