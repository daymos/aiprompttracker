Frequently Asked Questions

What's the cheapest Ahrefs alternative?

Ubersuggest at $29/month is the cheapest full-featured alternative with keyword research, rank tracking, and backlink analysis. For even tighter budgets, combine free tools like Google Search Console + Google Keyword Planner.

Is there a free alternative to Ahrefs?

Not with all features, but you can use Google Search Console (free, your own rankings), Google Keyword Planner (free with Google Ads account, search volumes), and Ubersuggest's free plan (3 searches/day) for basic keyword research.

Which tool has better backlink data than Ahrefs?

None‚ÄîAhrefs has the largest and most frequently updated backlink database. Majestic comes closest with unique Trust Flow metrics, and SEMrush is also competitive, but Ahrefs is generally considered #1 for backlink data.

Can I switch from Ahrefs without losing my data?

Yes! Export your tracked keywords and rankings from Ahrefs (CSV format), then import them into your new tool. Most alternatives like SE Ranking, Moz, and Keywords.chat support CSV imports for rank tracking projects.

Which Ahrefs alternative is best for beginners?

Keywords.chat (chat-based, zero learning curve) or Ubersuggest (simple dashboard, affordable). Both are designed for non-technical users, unlike Ahrefs which assumes you're already an SEO expert.
