Best FREE Tools (100% Free Forever)

4. Google Trends ‚Äì Best for Trend Analysis

5. Answer The Public ‚Äì Best for Question Keywords

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a 100% free alternative to Ahrefs?

Not a single tool, but you can combine Google Search Console (your rankings), Google Keyword Planner (search volumes), Google Trends (trends), and Ubersuggest Free (3 daily searches) to get 70% of Ahrefs' functionality for free.

What's the cheapest paid Ahrefs alternative?

Ubersuggest at $29/month is the cheapest full-featured alternative with unlimited keyword research, rank tracking, backlinks, and site audits. It has less data than Ahrefs, but it's perfect for small sites and solo founders.

Can I use Google Search Console instead of Ahrefs?

Partially. Google Search Console is free and excellent for tracking YOUR site's performance, but it doesn't offer competitor research, backlink analysis from other sites, or keyword suggestions. Use GSC for your own site + Ubersuggest ($29/mo) for competitor insights.

How accurate is Google Keyword Planner compared to Ahrefs?

Google Keyword Planner is 100% accurate because it comes directly from Google. However, it shows broad ranges ("10K-100K") unless you're actively running Google Ads. Ahrefs provides exact numbers but they're estimates based on click-through rates.

Is Ubersuggest as good as Ahrefs?

No‚ÄîAhrefs has a much larger backlink database and more accurate data. But Ubersuggest gives you 80% of the functionality at 22% of the cost ($29 vs $129). For most indie hackers, Ubersuggest is more than enough.
