2. Ahrefs

$129/month

Ahrefs is SEMrush's main competitor and offers nearly identical features with what many consider a cleaner interface.

Best for: SEO professionals who need the most accurate backlink data

Key Features:

  • Industry-leading backlink index (440+ billion pages)
  • Comprehensive keyword research
  • Content explorer for finding top-performing content
  • Rank tracking and site audit

Why it's like SEMrush: Nearly feature-for-feature equivalent, with some saying Ahrefs has better backlink data.

3. Moz Pro

$99/month

Moz was one of the original SEO tools and still has a loyal following, especially for their Domain Authority metric.

Best for: SEO agencies who need client reporting and white-label options

Key Features:

  • Domain Authority (DA) metric (industry standard)
  • Keyword research and tracking
  • Site crawl and technical SEO audit
  • Link explorer for backlink analysis

Why it's like SEMrush: Full-featured SEO platform with similar tools, though some data is less comprehensive.

4. Mangools (KWFinder)

$49/month

Mangools offers a suite of 5 simple SEO tools with beautiful interfaces. KWFinder is their keyword research tool.

Best for: Bloggers and content creators focused on finding easy-to-rank keywords

Key Features:

  • KWFinder (keyword research with difficulty scores)
  • SERPChecker (SERP analysis)
  • SERPWatcher (rank tracking)
  • LinkMiner (backlink checker)
  • SiteProfiler (competitor analysis)

Why it's like SEMrush: Covers all core SEO needs in a more user-friendly package at 1/3 the price.

5. SE Ranking

$65/month

SE Ranking is an underrated all-in-one platform popular with agencies for its white-label reporting.

Best for: Freelancers and agencies who need white-label client reports

Key Features:

  • Backlink checker and monitor
  • White-label PDF reports
  • Marketing plan builder

Why it's like SEMrush: Comprehensive feature set at half the price, with agency-friendly reporting.

6. SpyFu

$39/month

SpyFu specializes in competitor research, showing you exactly what keywords your competitors rank for and buy ads on.

Best for: Competitive analysis and PPC research

Key Features:

  • Competitor keyword research

  • SERP analysis
  • Historical data (up to 16 years)

Why it's like SEMrush: Strong competitor analysis features, especially for PPC campaigns.

7. Serpstat

$59/month

Serpstat is an affordable all-in-one tool with a focus on international SEO and multiple databases.

Best for: International businesses doing SEO in multiple countries

Key Features:

  • Keyword research (30+ country databases)

  • Rank tracking

8. Ubersuggest

$29/month

  • Keyword research and suggestions
  • Domain overview and traffic estimates
  • Backlink data
  • Site audit (basic)
  • Chrome extension

Why it's like SEMrush: Covers the basics at the lowest price point, perfect for learning SEO.

10. Majestic

SEMrush is an excellent tool, but it's overkill for most small businesses and indie hackers. Unless you're:

Frequently Asked Questions

What tool is most similar to SEMrush?

Ahrefs is the most similar with nearly identical features and pricing ($129/mo vs $140/mo). If you want similar functionality at a lower price, SE Ranking ($65/mo) or Serpstat ($59/mo) offer comprehensive platforms at 40-60% less cost.

Are there tools better than SEMrush?

"Better" depends on your needs. Ahrefs has superior backlink data. SpyFu has better competitor PPC intelligence. Keywords.chat is simpler to use. For most indie hackers, a specialized tool that fits your workflow is "better" than SEMrush's everything-for-everyone approach.

How much does SEMrush cost compared to alternatives?

SEMrush costs $140/month. Alternatives range from $29/mo (Ubersuggest) to $129/mo (Ahrefs). You can save $500-1,300/year by switching to a cheaper alternative with similar core features.

Can I switch from SEMrush to another tool without losing data?

Yes! Export your tracked keywords and rankings from SEMrush (CSV export), then import them into your new tool. Most tools like Ahrefs, Moz, and SE Ranking support CSV imports for rank tracking projects.

Which SEMrush alternative is best for beginners?

Keywords.chat (chat-based, no learning curve) or Ubersuggest (simple interface, affordable). Both are designed for non-technical users, unlike SEMrush which assumes SEO expertise.
