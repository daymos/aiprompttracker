Frequently asked questions

How does AI Prompt Tracker work?
We run your keywords and brand terms through multiple AI models (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity) to see when and how they mention your brand. You get detailed reports showing your visibility score, mention frequency, and competitive positioning.
Which AI models do you track?
Currently we track ChatGPT (OpenAI), Google Gemini, and Perplexity. We're constantly adding new models based on user demand.
How often are scans performed?
Pro users get daily automated scans. Free users can run 3 manual scans per month. You can trigger additional scans anytime on the Pro plan.
Can I track my competitors?
Yes! Add competitor domains to your project and see how you rank against them in AI responses. Great for competitive intelligence.
What's a visibility score?
It's a 0-100 score showing how prominent your brand is in AI responses. Factors include mention frequency, ranking position, sentiment, and relevance across different prompts and models.
Do you offer a free trial?
Yes! The Free plan includes 3 scans per month forever. Pro plan includes a 14-day free trial with full access to all features.
